The Building Process:

1. Budget

Your budget and loan preapproval amount will influence where you want to build, the size of your home, and the finishes of your home.
2. Location

Do you own land that you want to build on? Great! We can build on your property. If you do not own land, you can select from our available lots. Contact us for an updated list of lots.

3. Design

You can work with our in-house architect to begin designing your home. From floor plan to selecting finishes to finalizing your loan, we will guide you through the process. You will have access to all our vendors and will get our builders discount where available. Vendors seek out our business because of our reputation and on-time payments. If you already have an architect in mind, we will work with any architect you choose. Already have the floorplan and design? You are two steps ahead! Send us your floor plan, build of materials, survey, and other information and we will give you a cost estimate.

4. Build

We will work with the city permitting offices, homeowners’ associations, and all relevant parties to get approval to begin construction. We will oversee the building process through completion. You can participate as much as you'd like or just observe the process. We will seek your approval before making any changes not in our original plan.

5. Move In

Move into your new space and then stay connected! Your home will be covered under our warranty and we will be available to answer any follow-up questions or concerns.
